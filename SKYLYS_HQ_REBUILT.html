Competitors, trends, what's working in the industry, and what Skyly's does differently.

Research starting points

Look into Chipotle (customisable, fast), Ponzu, Atis, Cali-Press, and local bowl spots. What are they charging? What's the vibe? What's missing? Where does Skyly's fit and beat them?

Price benchmarks

Average McDonald's meal: ~£7–9. Average "healthy" bowl place: £10–14. Skyly's target: £6–9. That's the sweet spot — genuinely undercutting healthy food while matching fast food on price.

Allergens & Dietary ⚠️

The UK's 14 major allergens you must declare by law. Use these labels when building your menu so customers always know what's in their bowl. Tap a card to mark which allergens your kitchen currently uses.

Legal requirement (Natasha's Law)

As a food business in England you must clearly label all 14 allergens — either on packaging or a clearly visible menu board. Train yourself on this now so it's built into Skyly's from day one. It protects your customers and your business.
